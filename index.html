Trends in Production and Consumption of Oil, with Price Factors

A Deep dive into the demographics of oil production and consumption. An essential commodity, a driving force behind many economies.

Production Variations Across Countries

Big variations in production can be seen across countries, and even within a country between time periods. Many factors may influence this, such as demand, pricing, production capacity, and other market tactics.

Global Distribution Over Time

Global oil production appears to be concentrated in a few key countries in earlier years, diversifying in the later years. Key players across time include the United States, Saudi Arabia and Russia.

Data is missing for several countries from 2016 - 2024, hence the grey patches

Key Producers and Consumers

The oil market has been diversified by more players over time. Historically, much of the world's production and consumption has been led by a few key market players with strong economies. As the world has modernised, production and consumption has been more evenly split between more countries.

North America was consistently a top producer in the early 1900s, leading the world in the adoption of the automobile. Industrial output during the conflicts following would have also maintained this demand. However, as oil became a commodity sought by all, it's production was increased around the world.

Net Importers vs. Net Exporters

A log-log representation of production vs. consumption of oil. Countries below the line are net importers, while countries above the line are net exporters. European countries are generally net importers, while many Asian countries, particularly those in the Middle East, are net exporters.

Oil Prices and Production Connection

The relationship between oil price and production is such that small overall changes in production create big fluctuations in price. This may be explained by the world's relatively inelastic demand for petroleum products, meaning small fluctuations in production can have larger impacts on price.

Price and Production by Country

A more detailed breakdown for the correlation between production and pricing by individual country. Any directional pattern between the light and dark coloured dots indicates a relationship between price and oil production for that country.
